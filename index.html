About JAFRAS

Our Mission

JAFRAS is dedicated to disseminating high-quality scientific research across all disciplines. We believe in open access to academic knowledge and promote interdisciplinary collaboration.

Open Access

All articles published in JAFRAS are freely accessible. We are committed to science that is accessible to everyone and free from financial barriers.

Peer Review

Every submission undergoes a rigorous double-blind peer review process by international experts to ensure the highest scientific standards.

Publication Process

1

Submission

Submit your manuscript through our contact information. Ensure it complies with our formatting guidelines.

2

Initial Review

The editorial team reviews your paper for completeness, relevance, and alignment with our scope. Feedback within 5 business days.

3

Peer Review

Your manuscript is sent to at least two independent reviewers. The review process typically takes 4-6 weeks.

4

Revision & Decision

You receive the reviews and can submit a revised version if needed. The final decision is made after revision.

5

Publication

Upon acceptance, your paper is formatted, receives a DOI, and is published in the next available issue.
